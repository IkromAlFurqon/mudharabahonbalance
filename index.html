What is Mudharabah On Balance Sheet?

Mudharabah Muqayyadah on-balance-sheet is an agreement where the investor invests their money with a Shariah-compliant bank for management and subsequent distribution to the business executor according to specific conditions. This type of financing is typically used for longer-term projects or investments where the investor and the entrepreneur want to establish a formal partnership.





Product of Mudharabah On Balance Sheet

  1. Deposito: This refers to a type of investment deposit in which the depositor entrusts their funds to a financial institution for a fixed period.
  2. Tabungan: This refers to a savings account that operates on the principle of profit-sharing. The depositor entrusts their funds to a financial institution, which then invests the funds in sharia-compliant ventures.
  3. Obligation: This refers to an Islamic bond that is issued based on the mudharabah principle.
